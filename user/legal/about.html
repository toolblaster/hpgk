Our Mission & Vision

Founded under the ToolBlaster EdTech banner, HPGK Quiz was built to solve a critical challenge faced by Himachal Pradesh state competitive exam candidates: accessing affordable, high-quality, bilingual mock tests and topic-wise practice modules.

Whether you are preparing for HPAS (Himachal Administrative Services), HPPSC Allied Services, HP Patwari, HP Police Constable, HRTC Conductor, or Naib Tehsildar examinations, our platform delivers real-time exam simulation, instant accuracy analytics, and detailed bilingual solutions.

Freemium Model & Payment Transparency

We believe core educational practice should remain accessible. HPGK Quiz operates on a transparent freemium structure:

  • Free Access Quotas: Anonymous guests and free registered users enjoy free question quotas across all topic series and introductory full-length mock tests.
  • Paid Subscriptions: Power users seeking unlimited question practice, official PYQ banks, and full-length exam series can upgrade to the MCQ Pro Pass (₹39/month) or Mock Master Pass (₹89/month).
  • Secure Checkout: Payments are processed via encrypted 256-bit Razorpay checkout. Access passes are instantly provisioned on the server via automated payment verification.
  • Ad-Supported Content: To maintain server infrastructure and free content offerings, our platform displays third-party advertisements (such as Google AdSense).
