Data Protection & Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective for hpgk.toolblaster.com

1. Overview & Commitment

Welcome to HPGK Quiz Platform (hpgk.toolblaster.com), published by ToolBlaster EdTech. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share while using our bilingual competitive exam preparation platform, mock test simulator, and agriculture learning modules.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how it is used to save your test progress, how payments are securely processed, and your choices regarding third-party advertising cookies.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you interact with our platform (as an anonymous guest or authenticated user), we collect the following types of information:

  • Anonymous Guest Data: You may explore free question quotas without creating an account. In guest mode, no personal identifiable information (PII) is collected or stored on our servers.
  • Account Authentication Data (Google Sign-In): When you register or log in using Google OAuth, we receive basic public profile information provided by Google, including your full name, email address, and profile picture URL. This data is used solely to authenticate your account, maintain your quiz score history, and display your XP on the global leaderboard.
  • Quiz Performance & Score Logs: We store test attempt scores, category completion status, time taken per test, and accuracy metrics linked to your account ID so you can review detailed solutions and track preparation progress across devices.
  • Payment Transaction Metadata: When purchasing access passes (such as the MCQ Pro Pass or Mock Master Pass), payment transactions are processed securely through Razorpay. We receive and store transaction metadata including Razorpay Payment IDs, pass subscription start/expiry dates, and plan identifiers. We do NOT receive, handle, or store full credit/debit card numbers, CVV codes, net banking credentials, or UPI PINs on our servers.

3. Local & Session Storage Usage

We utilize standard browser LocalStorage and SessionStorage mechanisms to ensure a responsive, zero-delay exam experience:

  • Quiz State Caching: Your active question choices and bookmarks are cached locally on your device to prevent progress loss during internet network fluctuations.
  • Theme Preferences: Your selected Light/Dark visual theme is stored locally to maintain consistent visual appearance.
  • Broadcast Dismissal Flags: Session flags are set when you dismiss announcement popups to ensure you are not repeatedly interrupted while studying.
Control over Local Storage: You can clear your browser cache and local storage at any time through your browser settings. Note that clearing local cache while logged out will erase unsynced local guest progress.

4. Google AdSense & Third-Party Cookies

To support server maintenance, content creation, and keep our core practice modules accessible for students, HPGK Quiz displays third-party advertisements served by Google AdSense.

  • Use of Cookies: Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.
  • Advertising Cookies (DART Cookie): Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Opting Out of Personalized Ads: Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of third-party vendor cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

5. Trusted Third-Party Services

We partner with trusted third-party infrastructure providers to deliver secure authentication, database synchronization, and payment processing:

  • Google Firebase (Auth & Cloud Firestore): Manages secure OAuth 2.0 user authentication, cloud database syncing, and global leaderboard rankings.
  • Razorpay Software Private Limited: Encrypted 256-bit payment gateway handling subscription billing and instant pass provisioning.
  • Google Analytics: Aggregated, non-personally identifiable site analytics to monitor page traffic, user engagement, and platform performance.

6. Data Security Measures

We employ industry-standard technical safeguards to protect student data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using 256-bit Transport Layer Security (TLS/SSL).
  • Access Control: Administrative user management access is restricted to authorized personnel using multi-factor authentication.

7. Your Data Rights & Deletion Requests

You retain complete control over your account data. You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information linked to your Google profile on your Student Dashboard.
  • Request permanent deletion of your user profile, score logs, active passes, and leaderboard entries from our server database.

To submit an account or data deletion request, email us at hello@toolblaster.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" from your registered Google email address. Account purges are executed within 7 business days.
