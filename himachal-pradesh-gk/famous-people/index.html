Famous People of Himachal Pradesh

Detailed Biography Notes (विस्तृत जीवनी नोट्स)

Life and contributions of legends who shaped Himachal Pradesh.
(हिमाचल प्रदेश को आकार देने वाले दिग्गजों का जीवन और योगदान।)

1. Dr. Yashwant Singh Parmar (डॉ. वाई.एस. परमार)

'Founder of Himachal Pradesh' (Himachal Nirmata). First Chief Minister.
'हिमाचल निर्माता'। पहले मुख्यमंत्री।

  • Born: 4 Aug 1906 at Chanhalag, Sirmaur. Died: 2 May 1981.
  • Role: Led the Prajamandal movement. Instrumental in the formation of HP on 15 April 1948. CM for 4 terms (1952-1977).
  • Books Authored: 'Polyandry in the Himalayas', 'Himachal Pradesh: Its Proper Shape and Status', 'Himachal Pradesh: Case for Statehood'. (पुस्तकें: हिमालय में बहुपति प्रथा, आदि).
  • Legacy: UHF Nauni (Solan) is named after him. Known for his vision of horticulture and road connectivity.

2. Freedom Fighters (स्वतंत्रता सेनानी)

Leaders who fought against British and Princely rule.
ब्रिटिश और रियासती शासन के खिलाफ लड़ने वाले नेता।

  • Baba Kanshi Ram: Born in Dada Siba (Kangra). Known as 'Pahari Gandhi' (Title given by JL Nehru at Gadriwala, 1937) and 'Bulbul-e-Pahar' (by Sarojini Naidu). Vowed to wear black clothes until freedom. (पहाड़ी गांधी, बुलबुल-ए-पहाड़).
  • Pt. Padam Dev: Born in Rohru (Shimla). Known as 'Kaviraj'. Led the Suket Satyagraha (1948). Member of Constituent Assembly. (कविराज, sub सुकेत सत्याग्रह).
  • Bhai Hirda Ram: Born in Mandi. Associated with Ghadar Party. Sentenced to death (later life imprisonment) in Lahore Conspiracy Case. (गदर पार्टी, लाहौर षड्यंत्र).
  • Vaid Surat Singh: Leader of Pajhota Andolan (1942) in Sirmaur. Promoting non-violence. (पझोता आंदोलन).

3. Gallantry Award Winners (वीरता पुरस्कार विजेता)

Heroes who made the supreme sacrifice for the nation. HP is 'Veer Bhumi'.
राष्ट्र के लिए सर्वोच्च बलिदान देने वाले नायक। हिमाचल 'वीर भूमि' है।

  • Major Somnath Sharma (PVC): First recipient of Param Vir Chakra (Posthumous). Born in Kangra. Saved Srinagar airport in 1947 Indo-Pak war. (प्रथम परमवीर चक्र विजेता, 1947).
  • Captain Vikram Batra (PVC): 'Sher Shah' of Kargil. Born in Palampur. Famous quote: "Yeh Dil Maange More". Capturered Point 5140 & 4875. (शेर शाह, कारगिल, ये दिल मांगे मोर).
  • Rifleman Sanjay Kumar (PVC): Born in Bilaspur. Awarded PVC for bravery in Kargil War (Flat Top Area). Living legend. (संजय कुमार, कारगिल).
  • Subedar Major Sanjay Kumar (PVC): (Note: Often confused, check specific context). Major Dhan Singh Thapa (PVC - 1962 War). (मेजर धन सिंह थापा - 1962).

4. Artists & Writers (कलाकार और लेखक)

Creative minds who enriched the cultural heritage.
रचनात्मक व्यक्तित्व जिन्होंने सांस्कृतिक विरासत को समृद्ध किया।

  • Nicholas Roerich: Russian painter who settled in Naggar (Kullu). Famous for Himalayan landscapes. Roerich Art Gallery is in Naggar. (निकोलस रोरिक: रूसी चित्रकार, नग्गर).
  • Norah Richards: Irish-born actress. Settled in Andretta (Kangra). Known as 'Grandmother of Punjabi Theatre'. (नोरा रिचर्ड्स: पंजाबी रंगमंच की नानी, अंद्रेता).
  • Sardar Sobha Singh: Famous painter. Settled in Andretta. Famous works: 'Sohni Mahiwal', 'Kangra Bride', Portraits of Sikh Gurus. Art Gallery at Andretta. (सोभा सिंह: चित्रकार, सोहनी महिवाल).
  • Ruskin Bond: Born in Kasauli. Famous author. 'Room on the Roof'. (रस्किन बॉन्ड: कसौली में जन्म, लेखक).
  • Chandradhar Sharma Guleri: Writer from Guler (Kangra). Famous story 'Usne Kaha Tha'. (चंद्रधर शर्मा गुलेरी: 'उसने कहा था').

5. Reformers & Pioneers (समाज सुधारक और अग्रदूत)

Visionaries who brought social and economic change.
सामाजिक और आर्थिक परिवर्तन लाने वाले दूरदर्शी।

  • Satyanand Stokes: American missionary (Samuel Evans Stokes). Settled in Kotgarh. Introduced Delicious variety of Apples (1918), revolutionizing HP's economy. Built temple in Thanadhar. (सत्यानंद स्टोक्स: अमेरिकी, सेब क्रांति).
  • Rajkumari Amrit Kaur: Lived in Shimla (Manorville). First Health Minister of India. Founder of AIIMS. Gandhi's secretary. (राजकुमारी अमृत कौर: पहली स्वास्थ्य मंत्री, एम्स).
  • Bakshi Pratap Singh: Revolutionary from Kangra. Joined INA (Azad Hind Fauj). (बख्शी प्रताप सिंह: आजाद हिंद फौज).

6. Sports Personalities (खेल व्यक्तित्व)

Athletes who brought laurels to the state.
खिलाड़ी जिन्होंने राज्य का नाम रोशन किया।

  • The Great Khali (Dalip Singh Rana): Born in Dhiraina (Sirmaur). WWE wrestler and powerlifter. (द ग्रेट खली: सिरमौर, WWE).
  • Vijay Kumar: Born in Hamirpur. Shooter. Silver Medalist in 2012 London Olympics (25m Rapid Fire Pistol). (विजय कुमार: निशानेबाज, ओलंपिक रजत).
  • Samresh Jung: Shooter from Sirmaur. 'Goldfinger'. multiple CWG medals. (समरेश जंग: निशानेबाज).
  • Ajay Thakur: Kabaddi player from Nalagarh (Solan). Former Indian Captain. Arjuna Awardee. (अजय ठाकुर: कबड्डी).
  • Shiva Keshavan: Luge pilot from Manali. 6-time Olympian (Winter Olympics). (शिवा केशवन: ल्यूज, शीतकालीन ओलंपिक).

7. Religious & Spiritual Figures (धार्मिक)

Saints and sages associated with the land.
भूमि से जुड़े संत और ऋषि।

  • Dalai Lama (Tenzin Gyatso): 14th Dalai Lama. Settled in McLeodganj (Dharamshala) in 1959. Nobel Peace Prize (1989). (दलाई लामा: मैक्लोडगंज, नोबेल शांति पुरस्कार).
  • Baba Balak Nath: Sidh Purush. Cave temple at Deotsidh (Hamirpur). (बाबा बालक नाथ: दियोटसिद्ध).
  • Padmasambhava: 'Guru Rinpoche'. Brought Buddhism to Tibet/Himachal (8th Century). Rewalsar lake associated with him. (पद्मसंभव: बौद्ध धर्म प्रचारक, रिवालसर).

8. Key Political Leaders (प्रमुख राजनीतिक नेता)

Leaders who shaped modern Himachal.
आधुनिक हिमाचल को आकार देने वाले नेता।

  • Dr. Y.S. Parmar: First CM. (प्रथम मुख्यमंत्री).
  • Thakur Ram Lal: Second CM. From Jubbal-Kotkhai. (ठाकुर राम लाल).
  • Shanta Kumar: First Non-Congress CM (1977). Started 'Antyodaya' scheme. Writer ('Dharti Hai Balidan Ki'). (शांता कुमार: पहले गैर-कांग्रेसी सीएम, लेखक).
  • Virbhadra Singh: 'Raja Sahib' of Bushahr. 6-time Chief Minister. Longest serving CM. (वीरभद्र सिंह: 6 बार मुख्यमंत्री).
  • Prem Kumar Dhumal: 2-time CM. Promoted roads and education. (प्रेम कुमार धूमल).

9. Padma Awardees & Others (पद्म पुरस्कार विजेता)

Distinguished citizens honored by the nation.
राष्ट्र द्वारा सम्मानित प्रतिष्ठित नागरिक।

  • Vaio Kartar Singh: Padma Shri (Ayurveda). (वैद्य करतार सिंह).
  • Musafir Ram Bhardwaj: Padma Shri (Instrumental Folk Music - Pauna). (मुसाफिर राम भारद्वाज).
  • Vidyanand Saraik: Padma Shri (Literature & Folk Culture). (विद्यानंद सरैक).
  • Lal Chand Prarthi: 'Chand'. Politician and Writer ('Kulut Desh Ki Kahani'). (लाल चंद प्रार्थी: लेखक).
