Why This Matters? (यह महत्वपूर्ण क्यों है?)

Detailed Polity Notes (विस्तृत राजनीति नोट्स)

Comprehensive guide to the administrative structure of Himachal Pradesh.
(हिमाचल प्रदेश की प्रशासनिक संरचना का व्यापक गाइड।)

1. Administrative History (प्रशासनिक इतिहास)

The journey from a Chief Commissioner's Province to a full-fledged State.
(चीफ कमिश्नर प्रांत से पूर्ण राज्य तक की यात्रा।)

  • 15 April 1948: Chief Commissioner's Province. N.C. Mehta was the first Chief Commissioner. 30 Princely states merged. (चीफ कमिश्नर प्रांत, एनसी मेहता).
  • 1951: Part-C State under Part C States Act 1951. 36-member Legislative Assembly created. (पार्ट-सी राज्य, 36 सदस्य).
  • 1 March 1952: Major General Himmat Singh became the first Lt. Governor. (मेजर जनरल हिम्मत सिंह - पहले उपराज्यपाल).
  • 1 Nov 1956: Union Territory (UT) under States Reorganization Act. Vidhan Sabha replaced by Territorial Council. (केंद्र शासित प्रदेश, क्षेत्रीय परिषद).
  • 1 July 1963: Territorial Council converted back to Vidhan Sabha. (क्षेत्रीय परिषद वापस विधानसभा बनी).
  • 25 Jan 1971: 18th State of India. S. Chakravarti became the first Governor. (18वां राज्य, एस. चक्रवर्ती - पहले राज्यपाल).

2. Legislature (Vidhan Sabha) (विधायिका - विधानसभा)

Unicameral legislature. Initially 36 seats, increased to 68.
(एकसदनीय विधायिका। शुरुआत में 36 सीटें, अब 68।)

  • First Election: 1951. Congress won 24 seats. Dr. Y.S. Parmar became first CM (24 March 1952). (पहला चुनाव 1951, परमार पहले सीएम).
  • Vidhan Sabha Buildings: 
    • Council Chamber: Current Vidhan Sabha (Built 1925, inaugurated by Lord Reading). (काउंसिल चैंबर - वर्तमान विधानसभा)
    • Kennedy House: First temporary assembly. (कैनेडी हाउस - पहली अस्थायी)
  • Speakers: First Speaker: Jaiwant Ram (1952). First Woman Speaker: Vidya Stokes. (जयवंत राम, विद्या स्टोक्स).
  • Seats: Total 68. Kangra has max (15), Lahaul-Spiti/Kinnaur min (1 each). (कुल 68, कांगड़ा 15, लाहौल/किन्नौर 1).
  • E-Vidhan: HP Vidhan Sabha became India's first paperless assembly. (भारत की पहली कागज रहित विधानसभा).

3. Executive (Governor & CM) (कार्यपालिका)

The Governor is the constitutional head, CM is the real head.
(राज्यपाल संवैधानिक प्रमुख, मुख्यमंत्री वास्तविक प्रमुख।)

  • First Governor: S. Chakravarti (1971). (एस. चक्रवर्ती).
  • First Chief Minister: Dr. Y.S. Parmar (1952). Architect of HP. (डॉ. वाई.एस. परमार - हिमाचल निर्माता).
  • President's Rule: Imposed twice. 1977 (Shanta Kumar govt dismissed) & 1992 (Shanta Kumar govt dismissed post-Babri). (राष्ट्रपति शासन: 1977, 1992).
  • Secretariat: Ellerslie Building (Shimla) houses the HP Secretariat. (एलर्सली बिल्डिंग - सचिवालय).
  • Summer Capital: Dharamshala declared second capital (Winter Capital) in 2017. (धर्मशाला - शीतकालीन राजधानी).

4. Judiciary (High Court) (न्यायपालिका)

Guardian of the Constitution in the state.
(राज्य में संविधान का संरक्षक।)

  • HP High Court: Established on 25 Jan 1971. Located at Ravenswood, Shimla. (स्थापना: 1971, रेवेन्सवुड).
  • First Chief Justice: Justice M.H. Beg (Mirza Hameedullah Beg). (न्यायमूर्ति एम.एच. बेग).
  • First Woman Chief Justice: Justice Leila Seth (First woman CJ of any High Court in India). (लीला सेठ - भारत की पहली महिला CJ).
  • Judicial Commissioner's Court: Existed from 1948-1971. Replaced by High Court. (न्यायिक आयुक्त न्यायालय - 1948-71).
  • HP Administrative Tribunal (HPAT): Established 1986. First Chairman: Justice H.S. Thakur. Abolished and re-established multiple times. (प्रशासनिक न्यायाधिकरण - 1986).

5. Panchayati Raj & Local Bodies (पंचायती राज)

Three-tier system functioning effectively.
(त्रिस्तरीय प्रणाली प्रभावी रूप से कार्य कर रही है।)

  • HP Panchayati Raj Act: Passed in 1994 (Conformity with 73rd Amendment). Replaced 1968 Act. (1994 अधिनियम).
  • Structure: Gram Panchayat (Village), Panchayat Samiti (Block), Zila Parishad (District). (ग्राम पंचायत, पंचायत समिति, जिला परिषद).
  • Reservation: 50% reservation for women in Panchayats (First state to do so). (महिलाओं के लिए 50% आरक्षण).
  • Municipal Corporation: Shimla MC is one of the oldest in India (Est. 1851). Now 5 MCs: Shimla, Dharamshala, Solan, Mandi, Palampur. (शिमला एमसी - 1851, 5 नगर निगम).

6. Constitutional & Statutory Bodies (निकाय)

Key institutions ensuring transparency and recruitment.
(पारदर्शिता और भर्ती सुनिश्चित करने वाले प्रमुख संस्थान।)

  • HP Public Service Commission (HPPSC): Established 1971. Located at Nigam Vihar, Shimla. First Chairman: Lt. Gen K.S. Katoch. (लोक सेवा आयोग - 1971, के.एस. कटोच).
  • HP Staff Selection Commission (HPSSC): Hamirpur (Est 1998). Dissolved in 2023 due to irregularities. Replaced by Rajya Chayan Aayog. (कर्मचारी चयन आयोग - हमीरपुर, भंग).
  • Lokayukta: Act passed in 1983. First Lokayukta: Justice T.V.R. Tatachari. (लोकायुक्त - 1983, टीवीआर टाटाचारी).
  • HIPA: HP Institute of Public Administration. Located at Fairlawn, Shimla (1974). Training for civil servants. (हिपा - फेयरलॉन, 1974).

7. Official Residences (आधिकारिक निवास)

Historical buildings serving as residences for dignitaries.
(मान्यवरों के निवास के रूप में ऐतिहासिक इमारतें।)

  • Barnes Court: Official residence of the Governor (Raj Bhavan). Site of Simla Agreement (1972). (बार्न्स कोर्ट - राजभवन, शिमला समझौता).
  • Oakover: Official residence of the Chief Minister. (ओकओवर - मुख्यमंत्री निवास).
  • Peterhoff: Former Raj Bhavan (Burnt in 1981). Site of Nathuram Godse's trial (1948-49). Now a luxury hotel. (पीटरहॉफ - गोडसे मुकदमा).
  • Viceregal Lodge: Rashtrapati Niwas. Now Institute of Advanced Study. (वायसराय लॉज - राष्ट्रपति निवास).
  • Retreat Building (Mashobra): President of India's summer retreat. (रिट्रीट बिल्डिंग - राष्ट्रपति का ग्रीष्मकालीन आवास).

8. Police & Public Services (पुलिस और सार्वजनिक सेवाएं)

Law enforcement and essential services.
(कानून प्रवर्तन और आवश्यक सेवाएं।)

  • HP Police: Formed in 1948. First IGP: S.R. Chaudhary. HQ: Shimla. (हिमाचल पुलिस - 1948, एस.आर. चौधरी).
  • Right to Service Act: HP Public Service Guarantee Act 2011 ensures time-bound delivery of services. (लोक सेवा गारंटी अधिनियम 2011).
  • e-Governance: Him-Bhoomi (Land records), e-District (Certificates), CM Helpline (1100). (ई-गवर्नेंस - हिम-भूमि, सीएम हेल्पलाइन).

9. Administrative Structure (प्रशासनिक संरचना)

Hierarchy of administration for governance.
(शासन के लिए प्रशासन का पदानुक्रम।)

  • Divisions: 3 (Shimla, Mandi, Kangra). Mandi is the largest division. (3 मंडल - शिमला, मंडी, कांगड़ा).
  • Districts: 12. Largest: Lahaul-Spiti. Smallest: Hamirpur. (12 जिले).
  • Sub-Divisions & Tehsils: Multiple sub-divisions headed by SDM and Tehsils by Tehsildar. (उपमंडल और तहसीलें).
  • Blocks: Development Blocks for rural development. (विकास खंड).
