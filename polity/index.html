Detailed Polity Notes (विस्तृत राजनीति नोट्स)

Comprehensive guide to the political and administrative structure of Himachal Pradesh.
(हिमाचल प्रदेश की राजनीतिक और प्रशासनिक संरचना का व्यापक गाइड।)

1. Formation & Status (गठन और स्थिति)

The journey from a Chief Commissioner's Province to a full-fledged State.
चीफ कमिश्नर प्रांत से पूर्ण राज्य तक का सफर।

  • 15 April 1948: HP formed (30 Princely States). Chief Commissioner's Province. N.C. Mehta (First Chief Commissioner). E.P. Moon (Deputy). (1948 - गठन).
  • 1951: Part 'C' State under Lieutenant Governor. Major General Himmat Singh (First Lt. Governor - 1 March 1952). (1951 - पार्ट सी राज्य).
  • 1 Nov 1956: Union Territory. Bajrang Bahadur Singh (Lt. Governor). Territorial Council formed (Thakur Karam Singh - Chairman). (1956 - केंद्र शासित प्रदेश).
  • 1 Nov 1966: Punjab Reorganization. Area increased from 28,185 to 55,673 sq km. (1966 - पंजाब पुनर्गठन).
  • 25 Jan 1971: 18th State of India. S. Chakravarti (First Governor). (1971 - पूर्ण राज्यत्व).

2. State Legislature (राज्य विधायिका)

Evolution of the legislative assembly and historic venues.
विधानसभा का विकास और ऐतिहासिक स्थल।

  • First Assembly (1952): 36 Members. Dr. Y.S. Parmar (First CM). K.L. Mehta (First Chief Secretary). (1952 - 36 सदस्य).
  • Territorial Council (1957-63): Assembly replaced by Council. Reverted to Assembly in 1963 (UT with Legislature). (क्षेत्रीय परिषद).
  • Current Strength: 68 Constituencies (Since 1971-72 delimitation). 17 Reserved for SC, 3 for ST (Kinnaur, Lahaul-Spiti, Bharmour). (वर्तमान - 68 सीटें).
  • Buildings: Council Chamber (Built 1925) houses Vidhan Sabha now. Earlier meetings at Kennedy House. (काउंसिल चैंबर).
  • Speakers: Jaiwant Ram (First Speaker). Vidya Stokes (First Woman Speaker). (अध्यक्ष - जयवंत राम).

3. Judiciary (High Court) (न्यायपालिका)

Establishment of the High Court and judicial system.
उच्च न्यायालय और न्यायिक प्रणाली की स्थापना।

  • Formation: HP High Court established on 25 Jan 1971. Prior to this, it was under Delhi High Court (HP Bench). (स्थापना - 25 जनवरी 1971).
  • First Chief Justice: Justice M.H. Beg (Mirza Hameedullah Beg). (प्रथम मुख्य न्यायाधीश - एम.एच. बेग).
  • First Woman CJ: Justice Leela Seth (Also first woman CJ of a High Court in India). (प्रथम महिला मुख्य न्यायाधीश - लीला सेठ).
  • Location: Ravenswood, Shimla. (स्थान - रेवेन्सवुड).
  • Lokeyukta: Established 1983. Justice T.V.R. Tatachari (First Lokayukta). (लोकायुक्त - 1983).
  • HP Administrative Tribunal: Setup 1986, Abolished 2008, Re-established 2015, Abolished 2019. First Chairman: Justice H.S. Thakur. (प्रशासनिक न्यायाधिकरण).

4. Administrative Setup (प्रशासनिक ढांचा)

Divisions, Districts, and administrative hierarchy.
मंडल, जिले और प्रशासनिक पदानुक्रम।

  • Divisions (3): Shimla, Mandi, Kangra. Mandi is the largest division (5 districts). Kangra (3 districts), Shimla (4 districts). (मंडल - शिमला, मंडी, कांगड़ा).
  • Districts (12): Original 4 (Chamba, Mandi, Mahasu, Sirmour). Bilaspur added (1954). Kinnaur (1960). Kangra, Kullu, Lahaul-Spiti, Shimla (reorganized) added (1966). Una, Hamirpur, Solan carved out (1972). (12 जिले).
  • Headquarters: All district HQs are within district except Lahaul-Spiti (Keylong) and Kinnaur (Reckong Peo). (मुख्यालय).
  • Key Figures: First Chief Secretary: K.L. Mehta. First Governor: S. Chakravarti. First CM: Dr. Y.S. Parmar (4 terms). (प्रमुख व्यक्तित्व).

5. Local Self Government (स्थानीय स्वशासन)

Panchayati Raj Institutions (PRI) and Urban Local Bodies (ULB).
पंचायती राज संस्थाएं और शहरी स्थानीय निकाय।

  • Panchayati Raj Act: HP Panchayati Raj Act passed in 1968, then 1994 (aligned with 73rd Amendment). Three-tier system: Gram Panchayat, Panchayat Samiti, Zila Parishad. (पंचायती राज अधिनियम 1994).
  • Municipalities: Shimla Municipal Corporation (Oldest, 1852). Dharamshala, Solan, Mandi, Palampur are newer Corporations. (नगर निगम - शिमला सबसे पुराना).
  • Reservation: 50% reservation for women in PRIs (First state to do so). (महिलाओं के लिए 50% आरक्षण).
  • Key Facts: Maximum Panchayats in Kangra. Minimum in Lahaul-Spiti. (सर्वाधिक पंचायतें - कांगड़ा).

6. Constitutional Bodies (संवैधानिक निकाय)

Key organizations ensuring governance and recruitment.
शासन और भर्ती सुनिश्चित करने वाले प्रमुख संगठन।

  • HPPSC: Himachal Pradesh Public Service Commission. Established 1971. Location: Nigam Vihar, Shimla. First Chairman: Lt. Gen K.S. Katoch. (लोक सेवा आयोग - शिमला, के.एस. कटोच).
  • Election Commission: State Election Commission oversees Panchayat/Municipal elections. (राज्य चुनाव आयोग).
  • HPSSC (Dissolved): Hamirpur (Formerly SSSB). Dissolved in 2023 due to irregularities. Replaced by Rajya Chayan Aayog. (हमीरपुर चयन आयोग).
  • Language & Culture Academy: Established 1972 in Shimla. (भाषा और संस्कृति अकादमी).
  • HPA (HIPA): HP Institute of Public Administration, Fairlawn, Shimla (1974). First Director: B.K. Sharma. (हिपा - फेयरलॉन).

7. Important Acts & Legislation (महत्वपूर्ण अधिनियम)

Legislative milestones shaping the state.
राज्य को आकार देने वाले विधायी मील के पत्थर।

  • HP Big Landed Estates & Land Reforms Act, 1953: Abolished intermediaries. (भूमि सुधार अधिनियम).
  • HP Official Language Act, 1975: Hindi as official language. Pahari as associate. (राजभाषा अधिनियम - 1975).
  • HP Tenancy and Land Reforms Act, 1972: Provided land to the tiller. (काश्तकारी अधिनियम).
  • HP Public Service Guarantee Act, 2011: Timely delivery of services. (लोक सेवा गारंटी अधिनियम).
  • Anti-Conversion Law: Himachal Pradesh Freedom of Religion Act, 2019. (धर्मांतरण विरोधी कानून).

8. Elections & Political History (चुनाव और राजनीतिक इतिहास)

Key political events and figures.
प्रमुख राजनीतिक घटनाएं और व्यक्तित्व।

  • First Election (1951): Congress won. Dr. Parmar became CM. (प्रथम चुनाव 1951).
  • President's Rule: Imposed twice (1977, 1992). (राष्ट्रपति शासन).
  • Key CMs: Shanta Kumar (First non-Congress CM, 1977, Pani Wala CM). Virbhadra Singh (6 terms, 'Raja Sahib'). Prem Kumar Dhumal (Road network focus). (प्रमुख मुख्यमंत्री).
  • Lok Sabha Seats (4): Shimla (SC), Mandi, Kangra, Hamirpur. (लोकसभा सीटें - 4).
  • Rajya Sabha Seats (3): Elected by Vidhan Sabha. (राज्यसभा सीटें - 3).
  • First Voter: Shyam Saran Negi (Kalpa, Kinnaur) - First voter of Independent India (1951). (प्रथम मतदाता - श्याम सरन नेगी).

9. Administrative Institutes (प्रशासनिक संस्थान)

Training and research centers.
प्रशिक्षण और अनुसंधान केंद्र।

  • HIPA: Fairlawn, Shimla. Training for HAS officers. (हिपा).
  • Police Training College: Daroh (Kangra). (पुलिस प्रशिक्षण - डरोह).
  • Revenue Training Institute: Jogindernagar (Mandi). (राजस्व प्रशिक्षण).
  • Judicial Academy: Ghandal (Shimla). (न्यायिक अकादमी).
  • Agriculture University: CSKHPKV Palampur (1978). (कृषि विश्वविद्यालय).
  • Horticulture University: Dr. YSP UHF Nauni (Solan, 1985). (बागवानी विश्वविद्यालय).
