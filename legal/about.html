About HPGK Quiz

Empowering Aspirants with Knowledge

Our Mission

HPGK Quiz is a dedicated open-source initiative designed to help aspirants prepare for competitive examinations in Himachal Pradesh, including HPAS, HPPSC, Allied Services, and other state-level tests. Our goal is to provide comprehensive, accurate, and accessible study material in a user-friendly format.

What We Offer

  • Topic-wise Quizzes: Over 3000+ curated questions covering History, Geography, Economy, Culture, and more.
  • Bilingual Content: Questions and explanations are available in both English and Hindi to cater to a wider audience.
  • Detailed Notes: In-depth study notes for quick revision and concept clarity.
  • Progress Tracking: Local storage-based tracking to help you monitor your learning journey without the need for sign-ups.

The Team
