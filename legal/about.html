Our Mission

HPGK Quiz is a dedicated platform designed to help aspirants prepare for competitive examinations in Himachal Pradesh, including HPAS, HPPSC, and Allied Services. Our goal is to provide comprehensive and accessible study material in a modern format.

What We Offer

  • Topic-wise Quizzes: 3000+ curated questions covering History, Geography, and Culture.
  • Bilingual Content: Questions available in both English and Hindi simultaneously.
  • Detailed Notes: In-depth study notes and budget highlights for quick revision.
  • Privacy First: Local storage progress tracking without the need for sign-ups.
