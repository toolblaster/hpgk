Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. No Personal Data Collection

We respect your privacy. HPGK Quiz does not collect, store, or share any personal identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or location on our servers. You can use the application freely without creating an account.

2. Local Storage Usage

To enhance your experience, we use your browser's Local Storage technology. This allows us to:

  • Save your quiz progress (questions attempted, correct/incorrect answers).
  • Remember your theme preference (Dark/Light mode).
  • Store bookmarked questions for later review.

This data remains strictly on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. Clearing your browser cache will reset this data.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use standard analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to understand aggregate usage patterns (e.g., popular pages, total visitors) to improve the website. These tools may use cookies, but they do not identify you personally.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
