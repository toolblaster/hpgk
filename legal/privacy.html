Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. No Personal Data Collection

We respect your privacy. HPGK Quiz does not collect or share personal identifiable information (PII) such as name, email, or location. You can use the app freely without an account.

2. Local Storage Usage

To enhance your experience, we use Local Storage on your device to:

  • Save your quiz progress and history.
  • Remember your Dark/Light mode preference.
  • Store bookmarked questions for review.

This data remains strictly on your device and is never transmitted to us.

3. Analytics

We may use standard tools (like Google Analytics) to monitor aggregate usage patterns to improve the site. These tools do not identify you personally.

4. Policy Updates

Changes will be posted on this page and reflected by the "Last Updated" date.
