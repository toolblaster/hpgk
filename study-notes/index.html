HP Govt Welfare Schemes

Mastering latest initiatives is crucial for scoring well in the HP GK section. सरकारी योजनाओं को समझना एचपीएएस और अलाइड परीक्षाओं के लिए अत्यंत महत्वपूर्ण है।

  • Health & Welfare: HIMCARE, Sahara Yojana, and Pyari Behna Samman Nidhi.
  • Employment Focus: Mukhya Mantri Swavalamban Yojana and Rajiv Gandhi Startup schemes.

Economic Survey & Budget

Stay updated with the latest fiscal data and macroeconomic aggregates of Himachal Pradesh. नवीनतम राजकोषीय डेटा, GSDP और क्षेत्र-वार बजट आवंटन के साथ अपडेट रहें।

  • Statistical Data: Easy to memorize numbers on Per Capita Income and GSDP growth.
  • Sector Allocations: Breakdown of funds given to Education, Health, and Rural Development.

Revenue & Land Laws

The ultimate technical guide specifically tailored for HP Patwari and Naib Tehsildar exams. पटवारी और नायब तहसीलदार परीक्षाओं के लिए तैयार की गई विशेष तकनीकी मार्गदर्शिका।

  • Terminology: Understand complex Persian/Urdu terms like Jamabandi, Khasra, and Musavi.
  • Section 118: Clear rules regarding the transfer of land to non-agriculturists in HP.
  • Calculations: Standard conversions from Bigha and Biswa to Square Yards.

Agriculture & Horticulture

Detailed notes on the primary backbone of the state's economy, covering crop zones and livestock. राज्य की अर्थव्यवस्था की रीढ़ की हड्डी पर विस्तृत नोट्स, जिसमें कृषि क्षेत्र और पशुधन शामिल हैं।

  • Apple Economy: High-density rootstocks (M-9) and disease management like Apple Scab.
  • Natural Farming: Prakritik Kheti Khushhal Kisan Yojana (PK3Y).
  • Livestock: Key facts about indigenous breeds and veterinary care.
