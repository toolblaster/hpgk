Important Economic Fact-Checks (महत्वपूर्ण आर्थिक तथ्य)

Crucial pointers often asked as trick questions in the HPAS and HPPSC examinations.
(अक्सर हि.प्र. लोक सेवा आयोग परीक्षाओं में पूछे जाने वाले महत्वपूर्ण तथ्य।)

1. Sectoral Contribution & GSDP (क्षेत्रीय योगदान और GSDP)

  • Tertiary Sector (Services): The fastest-growing sector, contributing nearly 43.4% to HP's GVA, driven by IT, trade, transport, and tourism. (सेवा क्षेत्र GVA में लगभग 43.4% का योगदान देता है).
  • Secondary Sector: Accounts for ~43.1% of GVA, heavily dominated by Manufacturing (BBN pharma hub), Construction, and Hydropower generation. (विनिर्माण और पनबिजली के कारण द्वितीयक क्षेत्र 43.1%).
  • Primary Sector: Though employing ~57.03% of the workforce, it contributes only ~13.5% to GVA due to small marginal landholdings. (कृषि क्षेत्र का योगदान केवल 13.5% है).
  • Per Capita Income: Estimated at ₹2,35,199, which is significantly higher than the national average. (प्रति व्यक्ति आय राष्ट्रीय औसत से काफी अधिक है).
  • GSDP Growth: HP's real economic growth rate at constant (2011-12) prices is estimated around 7.1%. (स्थिर कीमतों पर विकास दर ~7.1%).

2. Banking, Credit & Inclusion (बैंकिंग, साख और समावेशन)

  • CD Ratio Challenge: HP's Credit-Deposit Ratio is low (around 34-36%), with the SLBC actively working to push credit flow past 40%. (राज्य का ऋण-जमा अनुपात 34-36% के आसपास कम है).
  • Lead Bank Allocation: PNB leads in 6 districts, UCO Bank in 4, while State Bank of India (SBI) acts as lead bank in Lahaul-Spiti & Sirmaur. (पीएनबी 6 और यूको 4 जिलों में लीड बैंक है).
  • Branch Density: HP boasts high banking penetration with 1 branch per ~3,000 population. (प्रति 3,000 आबादी पर एक बैंक शाखा).
  • Cooperative Network: HPSCB operates in 6 districts, KCCB in 5, and JCCB exclusively in Solan district. (HPSCB 6, KCCB 5 और JCCB सोलन में कार्य करता है).
  • Financial Inclusion: PMJDY zero-balance accounts combined with APY, PMJJBY, and PMSBY provide comprehensive safety nets. (जन धन और सामाजिक सुरक्षा बीमा योजनाएं).

3. Key Welfare Schemes (कल्याणकारी योजनाएं)

  • Sukh Ashray Yojana: State legally adopted orphans as 'Children of the State', granting ₹4000/month pocket money till 27 years and housing assistance. (अनाथों को 'राज्य के बच्चे' माना गया).
  • HIMCARE & Sahara: HIMCARE offers ₹5 Lakh cashless health cover for non-PMJAY families, while Sahara provides ₹3000/month for chronic illness treatment. (सहारा योजना में ₹3000/माह).
  • Sukh Samman Nidhi: Provides a dedicated ₹1500/month pension to women under the Pyari Behna Samman Nidhi scheme. (महिलाओं के लिए ₹1500/माह पेंशन).
  • Swaran Jayanti Nari Sambal: Grants ₹1150/month pension to women aged 65-69 years without any income threshold. (65-69 वर्ष की महिलाओं को बिना आय सीमा पेंशन).
  • Urban Employment: MMSAGY guarantees 120 days of wage employment in urban areas, surpassing national standards. (शहरी क्षेत्रों में 120 दिन का गारंटीकृत रोजगार).

4. Hydropower & Green Energy (जलविद्युत और हरित ऊर्जा)

  • Harnessable Potential: Out of the total estimated 27,436 MW potential, HP has harnessed ~11,150+ MW (~40% of state capacity). (कुल 27,436 MW क्षमता में से ~11,150+ MW का दोहन).
  • Basin Share: Satluj River Basin holds the maximum potential (~13,332 MW), followed by Beas (~5,995 MW) and Chenab (~4,032 MW). (सतलुज बेसिन में सबसे अधिक 13,332 MW क्षमता).
  • Green State Target: HP aims to become India's 1st 'Green Energy State' by March 2026 by adding 500 MW solar capacity. (मार्च 2026 तक 'हरित ऊर्जा राज्य' बनने का लक्ष्य).
  • Solar Expansion: SJVN is developing an 880 MW mega solar park in Spiti Valley, while Rajiv Gandhi Startup Phase-2 promotes 100-500 kW solar plants. (स्पीति में 880 MW सोलर पार्क).
  • Free Power & LADA: 125 units of free electricity for domestic users; 1.5% of project cost goes to LADA for local area development. (1.5% राशि LADA फंड में).

5. Tourism & Infrastructure (पर्यटन और अवसंरचना)

  • Tourism Capital: Kangra is being aggressively developed as the state's official 'Tourism Capital' with a Bankhandi Zoo and Golf Course. (कांगड़ा को 'पर्यटन राजधानी' के रूप में विकास).
  • Nai Raahein Nai Manzilein: State scheme focused on unexplored destinations like Janjehli (eco-tourism), Bir-Billing (paragliding), and Chanshal (skiing). (अनछुए गंतव्यों का विकास).
  • Cultural Tourism: ADB-funded $100 Million project is developing Shiv Dham in Mandi and beautifying Mall Road in Shimla. (मंडी में शिव धाम का विकास).
  • Road Network: Motorable road network exceeds 40,000 km, with PMGSY providing rural connectivity and HRTC adding electric buses. (40,000+ किमी मोटर योग्य सड़कें).
  • Rail & Air Expansion: Bhanupali-Bilaspur-Beri (63.1 km) & Chandigarh-Baddi (33.2 km) railway lines under construction; Mandi's Nagchala airport proposed. (रेल और हवाई कनेक्टिविटी विकास).

6. Budget & Fiscal Finances (बजट एवं राजकोषीय स्थिति)

  • Expenditure Focus: The Education sector consistently consumes the largest chunk (16-18%) of the state budget, followed by Health (6-7%). (बजट का सबसे बड़ा हिस्सा शिक्षा पर).
  • Committed Liabilities: Salaries (~25-26%) and Pensions (~16-17%) consume >42% of every rupee spent, creating a tight fiscal space. (वेतन और पेंशन राज्य का सबसे बड़ा खर्च है).
  • Revenue Sources: Share in Central Taxes (~25 paise) and Grants-in-aid (~20 paise) make up the largest part of every rupee earned. (केंद्रीय करों में हिस्सा कमाई का मुख्य जरिया).
  • Fiscal Deficit: HP aims to restrict its fiscal deficit around 4.5% to 4.7% of GSDP, slightly higher than FRBM targets. (राजकोषीय घाटा GSDP का 4.5% से 4.7%).
  • Old Pension Scheme (OPS): Restoration of OPS for government employees stands as a major social security and fiscal decision. (ओल्ड पेंशन स्कीम/OPS की बहाली).
