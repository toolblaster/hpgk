Frequently Asked Questions • 10 भूमि माप एवं टूल प्रश्नोत्तर
1. How many Sq. Yards (Gaj) equal 1 Bigha in HP?
In Himachal Pradesh, 1 standard Bigha equals exactly 968 Square Yards (809.37 Sq. Meters).
2. How many Biswa are in 1 Bigha?
1 Bigha is subdivided into 20 Biswa in standard HP revenue records.
3. How many Bighas equal 1 Acre in HP?
1 Acre is equal to 5 Bigha (or 4,840 Square Yards / 8 Kanals).
4. What is the length of 1 Karam in HP revenue units?
1 Karam measures 57.15 inches (4 Feet 9.15 Inches) in standard survey chains.
5. What is 1 Sarsahi in land area?
1 Sarsahi equals 1 square Karam (approximately 30.25 Square Feet). 9 Sarsahis equal 1 Marla.
6. Who is the highest revenue authority in HP?
The Financial Commissioner (Revenue) holds ultimate judicial & administrative revenue authority.
7. Who appoints Patwaris in HP revenue district?
The Deputy Commissioner (Collector) is the official appointing authority for Patwaris.
8. What is the main function of Himbhoomi portal?
It manages digitized Jamabandi records and generates official verified land ownership copies.
9. What is the ULPIN code structure?
ULPIN is a 14-digit alphanumeric code mapping the centroid latitude/longitude of a land parcel.
10. How does SVAMITVA scheme benefit villagers?
It conducts 5cm-accuracy drone surveys of Abadi Deh lands to issue bankable Property Cards.
