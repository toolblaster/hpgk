Economy of Himachal Pradesh

Why This Matters? (यह महत्वपूर्ण क्यों है?)

Economy is the most dynamic section of HP GK. Around 25% of questions in HPAS/Allied exams come from Economic Survey data, Budget, Hydro projects, and Agriculture.

Detailed Economy Notes (विस्तृत अर्थव्यवस्था नोट्स)

Comprehensive guide to the economic landscape of Himachal Pradesh.
(हिमाचल प्रदेश के आर्थिक परिदृश्य का व्यापक गाइड।)

1. Agriculture & Crop Production (कृषि और फसल उत्पादन)

Agriculture supports 70% of the population. Share in GSDP is ~13.5% (2022-23). The state has 9.44 lakh hectares of operational holdings.
70% जनसंख्या का मुख्य व्यवसाय कृषि है। GSDP में इसका योगदान ~13.5% है।

  • Major Crops: Maize (Highest production - 7.42 lakh MT), Wheat (6.67 lakh MT), Rice (1.16 lakh MT). Potato (Seed potato from Lahaul is virus-free and world-famous). (मक्का, गेहूं, चावल, आलू).
  • Tea Cultivation: Introduced by Dr. Jameson in Kangra (1849). 'Kangra Tea' got GI tag (2005) & European GI tag (2023). Grown in Palampur, Baijnath, Dharamshala. (कांगड़ा चाय - GI टैग, 1849).
  • Kuth & Hops: Kuth (Saussurea lappa) cultivation started in Lahaul-Spiti (1925). Hops (for beer) grown in Lahaul. Saffron in Kinnaur/Mandi. (कुठ, हॉप्स, केसर).
  • Cash Crops: Ginger (Sirmaur is the largest producer). Off-season vegetables (Tomato, Capsicum) in Solan/Shimla yield high returns. (अदरक - सिरमौर, बेमौसमी सब्जियां).
  • Key Schemes: Mukhya Mantri Khet Sanrakshan Yojana (80% subsidy for Solar Fencing), Prakritik Kheti Khushhal Kisan (Zero Budget Natural Farming - Subhash Palekar model). (सौर बाड़, प्राकृतिक खेती).

2. Horticulture & Floriculture (बागवानी और पुष्प उत्पादन)

Himachal is the 'Apple State of India' and 'Fruit Bowl'. Horticulture contributes approx ₹5000 Crore to the state economy annually.
'भारत का सेब राज्य' और 'फलों का कटोरा'।

  • Apple History: First British orchard by Capt. R.C. Lee (Kullu - 1870). First American (Delicious) variety introduced by Satyanand Stokes (Samuel Evans Stokes) at Kotgarh in 1918. Shimla leads in production. (सेब - 1870, 1918 सत्यानंद स्टोक्स).
  • Mushroom: Solan is the 'Mushroom City of India'. National Research Centre for Mushroom (NRCM) is located at Chambaghat, Solan. (खुम्ब शहर - सोलन).
  • Floriculture: Solan, Shimla, Sirmaur, and Kangra are hubs. Crops: Carnation, Gladiolus, Lilium, Marigold. 'Pushp Kranti Yojana' supports polyhouses. (पुष्प क्रांति योजना).
  • Projects: HPHDP (World Bank funded) for importing rootstocks. Sub-tropical Horticulture Irrigation Value Addition (SHIVA) project ($130M ADB loan) for lower hills (Orange, Guava). (SHIVA प्रोजेक्ट, विश्व बैंक).

3. Hydropower Potential (जलविद्युत क्षमता)

HP has five river basins with an identified potential of 27,436 MW. Harnessed capacity is ~10,500 MW. Known as the 'White Coal' of HP.
कुल क्षमता: 27,436 मेगावाट। दोहन: ~10,500 मेगावाट। 'सफेद कोयला'।

  • Bhakra Dam (1325 MW): On Satluj (Bilaspur). 'Temple of Modern India' (Nehru). Highest gravity dam (226m) at completion (1963). Joint venture of Punjab, Haryana, Rajasthan. (भाखड़ा बांध - 1963, 226m).
  • Nathpa Jhakri (1500 MW): On Satluj (Kinnaur/Shimla). Largest underground project in India. Executed by SJVN (Satluj Jal Vidyut Nigam). World Bank aided. (नाथपा झाकड़ी - SJVN, सबसे बड़ी भूमिगत).
  • Karcham Wangtoo (1091 MW): On Satluj (Kinnaur). Largest Private sector (JSW) project in HP. (करछम वांगतू - निजी क्षेत्र).
  • Giri Bata (60 MW): First project executed by HP Govt (1966) in Sirmaur district. (गिरी बाटा - पहली राज्य परियोजना).
  • HIMURJA: State nodal agency formed in 1989 for renewable energy (Solar, Micro-hydel). Target: Green Energy State by 2026. (हिमऊर्जा - 1989, हरित ऊर्जा लक्ष्य 2026).

4. Industrial Development (औद्योगिक विकास)

Shift from agrarian to industrial economy. The Baddi-Barotiwala-Nalagarh (BBN) belt is the primary industrial hub.
कृषि से औद्योगिक अर्थव्यवस्था की ओर। बीबीएन (BBN) मुख्य हब है।

  • Pharmaceuticals: Baddi (Solan) is known as the 'Pharma Hub of Asia'. Manufactures 35% of Asia's medicines. Major firms: Cipla, Dr. Reddy's. (फार्मा हब - बद्दी, 35% उत्पादन).
  • Cement Plants: Limestone deposits in Bilaspur, Solan, Sirmaur. Barmana (ACC - Bilaspur, 1984), Darlaghat (Ambuja - Solan, 1995), Rajban (CCI - Sirmaur - First plant 1980). (सीमेंट संयंत्र - राजबन 1980, बरमाणा, दाड़लाघाट).
  • Electronic Complex: Chambaghat (Solan). Software Technology Park (STPI) in Shimla. (इलेक्ट्रॉनिक कॉम्प्लेक्स - सोलन).
  • Industrial Areas: Mehatpur (Una), Kala Amb (Sirmaur), Paonta Sahib, Sansarpur Terrace (Kangra), Gwalthai (Bilaspur). (प्रमुख औद्योगिक क्षेत्र).
  • Policy: Ease of Doing Business - HP ranks well among hilly states. 'Mukhya Mantri Swavalamban Yojana' promotes startups. (ईज़ ऑफ डूइंग बिज़नेस, स्वावलंबन योजना).

5. Tourism Sector (पर्यटन क्षेत्र)

A major contributor to GDP (approx 7%). Tagline: 'Unforgettable Himachal'. Focus on Sustainable and Eco-Tourism.
जीडीपी में ~7% योगदान। 'अविस्मरणीय हिमाचल'।

  • Religious Tourism: Shakti Peeths (Jwalamukhi, Naina Devi, Chintpurni, Chamunda), Baijnath (Shiva), Bhimakali (Sarahan), Tabo Monastery (Lahaul). (धार्मिक - शक्तिपीठ, ताबो).
  • Adventure Tourism: Paragliding (Bir Billing - World's 2nd best site), Skiing (Solang, Kufri, Narkanda), River Rafting (Beas - Kullu, Tattapani - Satluj). (साहसिक - पैराग्लाइडिंग, स्कीइंग, राफ्टिंग).
  • Heritage Tourism: Kalka-Shimla Railway (UNESCO World Heritage), Kangra Fort (Nagarkot), Naggar Castle, Vice Regal Lodge (Shimla). (विरासत - कालका-शिमला रेलवे, कांगड़ा किला).
  • Eco-Tourism: Great Himalayan National Park (UNESCO - Kullu), Tirthan Valley. 'Nai Raahein Nai Manzilein' scheme to develop unexplored spots (Janjheli, Chanshal, Bir Billing). (इको-पर्यटन, नई राहें नई मंजिलें).
  • Atal Tunnel: New tourist attraction. 9.02 km long, world's longest highway tunnel above 10,000 ft. Connects Manali to Lahaul. (अटल टनल - नया आकर्षण).

6. Animal Husbandry & Fisheries (पशुपालन और मत्स्य पालन)

Livestock is integral to the rural economy. 19th Livestock Census: Total livestock 48.44 lakh.
पशुपालन ग्रामीण अर्थव्यवस्था का अभिन्न अंग है।

  • Sheep Breeding Farms: Jeori (Shimla), Tal (Hamirpur), Karcham (Kinnaur), Sarol (Chamba). HP Wool Fed established in 1988. Migratory shepherds (Gaddis) rear Gaddi sheep. (भेड़ प्रजनन केंद्र - ज्यूरी, ताल, करछम, सरोल).
  • Angora Rabbit Farms: Kandwari (Kangra), Nagwain (Mandi). For high-quality wool. (अंगोरा खरगोश फार्म - कंडवाड़ी, नगवाईं).
  • Yak & Horse Breeding: Yak Breeding Farm at Lari (Lahaul-Spiti). Horse Breeding Farm at Lari for Chamurthi Horse ('Ship of Cold Desert'). (याक और घोड़ा प्रजनन - लारी).
  • Milk Production: HP Milkfed ('Shimla', 'Him' brand). Murrah buffalo and Jersey cow improvement programs. Kamdhenu Hitkari Manch (Bilaspur). (दुग्ध उत्पादन - मिल्कफेड).
  • Fisheries: Trout farming in Kullu/Mandi (Indo-Norwegian project). Carp farming in Bilaspur/Una. Masheer in Pong Dam. (ट्राउट और कार्प मछली पालन).

7. Transport & Communication (परिवहन और संचार)

Lifelines of the hills. Roads are the primary mode. Road density highest in Hamirpur, lowest in Lahaul-Spiti.
पहाड़ों की जीवन रेखा। हमीरपुर में सड़क घनत्व सबसे अधिक।

  • Roads: HRTC formed in 1974. Total road length ~40,000 km. NH-5 (Hindustan-Tibet Road) and NH-3 (Leh-Manali) are strategic. (सड़कें - HRTC 1974, NH-5, NH-3).
  • Railways: Kalka-Shimla (96 km, 103 tunnels, built 1903, UNESCO status 2008), Pathankot-Jogindernagar (Narrow gauge, 113 km, built 1929 for Shanan power house), Una-Nangal (Broad gauge). (रेलवे - कालका-शिमला 1903).
  • Airports: Jubbar Hatti (Shimla - 22km from city), Bhuntar (Kullu - 10km), Gaggal (Kangra - Largest in HP). Expansion proposed for Gaggal. (हवाई अड्डे - जुब्बर हट्टी, भुंतर, गग्गल).
  • Ropeways: Jakhu (Shimla), Dharamshala-McLeodganj. 'Parvat Mala' scheme for new ropeways (Bijli Mahadev, Palchan-Rohtang). (रोपवे - जाखू, पर्वत माला).

8. Planning & Economic Survey (योजना और आर्थिक सर्वेक्षण)

Key indicators and government initiatives based on the latest Economic Survey.
नवीनतम आर्थिक सर्वेक्षण पर आधारित प्रमुख संकेतक और सरकारी पहल।

  • Growth Rate: HP GSDP growth estimated at ~6.4% (2022-23). Per Capita Income is approx ₹2.22 Lakh (Higher than national average). (विकास दर ~6.4%, उच्च प्रति व्यक्ति आय).
  • Social Security: 'Old Age Pension' age lowered to 60 years for all (no income limit). 'Indira Gandhi Pyari Behna Sukh-Samman Nidhi' (₹1500/month). (सामाजिक सुरक्षा - वृद्धावस्था पेंशन, सुख-सम्मान निधि).
  • Flagship Schemes: Himcare (Health insurance up to 5 Lakh), Sahara (Financial aid for serious illness ₹3000/month), Grihini Suvidha (Free LPG for smoke-free state), Beti Hai Anmol. (प्रमुख योजनाएं - हिमकेयर, सहारा, गृहिणी सुविधा).
  • SDG Index: HP consistently ranks in Top 3 in NITI Aayog SDG India Index (Strong in Goal 4: Quality Education, Goal 6: Clean Water). (सतत विकास लक्ष्य - शीर्ष 3 में).

9. Banking & Co-operatives (बैंकिंग और सहकारिता)

Financial inclusion and rural credit backbone. Cooperative movement started in Una.
वित्तीय समावेशन और ग्रामीण ऋण। ऊना में सहकारी आंदोलन की शुरुआत।

  • Lead Bank: UCO Bank (United Commercial Bank) holds the lead bank responsibility in 10 districts of HP. PNB in 2 districts. (लीड बैंक - यूको बैंक).
  • Kangra Central Co-op Bank (KCCB): Largest cooperative bank in HP. HQ: Dharamshala. (कांगड़ा को-ऑप बैंक - सबसे बड़ा).
  • Jogindra Central Co-op Bank: Solan. First registered cooperative society in HP (1892) was at Panjawar (Una). (जोगिंद्रा बैंक - पंजवर में पहली समिति).
  • H.P. State Co-operative Bank: Apex bank. HQ: Shimla. Has branches even in remote Dodra-Kwar. (राज्य सहकारी बैंक - शिमला).
  • Banking Network: Over 2244+ bank branches. High CD (Credit-Deposit) Ratio indicates good lending. (बैंकिंग नेटवर्क).
