Detailed Personalities Notes (विस्तृत व्यक्तित्व नोट्स)

Biographies and achievements of legendary figures from Himachal Pradesh.
(हिमाचल प्रदेश की महान हस्तियों की जीवनी और उपलब्धियां।)

1. Freedom Fighters (स्वतंत्रता सेनानी)

Leaders who fought against British rule and feudalism.
ब्रिटिश शासन और सामंतवाद के खिलाफ लड़ने वाले नेता।

  • Dr. Y.S. Parmar: 'Founder of Himachal Pradesh'. First CM (1952). Led Prajamandal movement. Born: Chanhalag (Sirmaur). (डॉ. परमार - हिमाचल निर्माता).
  • Pt. Padam Dev: 'Kavi Raj'. Led Suket Satyagraha (1948). Revolutionary poet. Born: Rohru (Shimla). (पं. पदम देव - सुकेत सत्याग्रह).
  • Baba Kanshi Ram: 'Pahari Gandhi' (Title by JL Nehru) & 'Bulbul-e-Pahar' (Title by Sarojini Naidu). Vowed to wear black clothes till independence. Born: Dada Siba (Kangra). (बाबा कांशी राम - पहाड़ी गांधी).
  • Bhagmal Sautha: Leader of Dhami Firing tragedy (1939). Founding member of Shimla Prajamandal. (भागमल सौठा - धामी गोलीकांड).

2. Defense Heroes (रक्षा नायक)

Braves who received the highest gallantry awards.
सर्वोच्च वीरता पुरस्कार प्राप्त करने वाले वीर।

  • Major Somnath Sharma: First recipient of Param Vir Chakra (Posthumous, 1947). Defended Srinagar airport. Born: Dadh (Kangra). (मेजर सोमनाथ शर्मा - प्रथम PVC).
  • Captain Vikram Batra: 'Sher Shah' of Kargil. PVC (Posthumous, 1999). Famous quote "Yeh Dil Maange More". Born: Palampur (Kangra). (कैप्टन विक्रम बत्रा - शेर शाह).
  • Rifleman Sanjay Kumar: PVC (Living, 1999). Captured Flat Top point in Kargil. Born: Bilaspur. (संजय कुमार - PVC).
  • Subedar Major Bhandari Ram: Victoria Cross (1944). Burma Campaign. Born: Bilaspur. (भंडारी राम - विक्टोरिया क्रॉस).

3. Artists & Painters (कलाकार और चित्रकार)

Guardians of Himachali art and culture.
हिमाचली कला और संस्कृति के संरक्षक।

  • Sardar Sobha Singh: Famous painter. Art gallery at Andretta (Kangra). Masterpiece: 'Sohni Mahiwal', 'Guru Nanak Dev'. (शोभा सिंह - अंद्रेटा).
  • Nicholas Roerich: Russian artist. Settled in Naggar (Kullu). Painted Himalayas. Roerich Art Gallery. (निकोलस रोरिख - कुल्लू).
  • Norah Richards: 'Grandmother of Punjabi Theatre'. Lived in Andretta. Promoted drama and arts. (नोरा रिचर्ड्स - अंद्रेटा).
  • Maheshwar Singh: Miniature painter of Chamba school. Padmashri awardee. (महेश्वर सिंह - चंबा शैली).

4. Writers & Poets (लेखक और कवि)

Literary giants from the hills.
पहाड़ों के साहित्यिक दिग्गज।

  • Chandradhar Sharma Guleri: Story: 'Usne Kaha Tha' (Flashback technique). Editor of 'Samalochak'. Born: Jaipur (Family from Guler, Kangra). (चंद्रधर शर्मा गुलेरी - उसने कहा था).
  • Yashpal: Revolutionary writer. Member of HSRA. Works: 'Jhutha Sach', 'Dada Comrade'. Lived in Hamirpur. (यशपाल - झूठा सच).
  • Ruskin Bond: Born in Kasauli (1934). Famous author of children's books. 'The Room on the Roof'. (रस्किन बॉन्ड - कसौली).
  • Nirmal Verma: Jnanpith Awardee (1999). Born in Shimla. Work: 'Parinde'. (निर्मल वर्मा - ज्ञानपीठ).

5. Political Leaders (राजनीतिक नेता)

Statesmen who shaped modern Himachal.
आधुनिक हिमाचल को आकार देने वाले राजनेता।

  • Virbhadra Singh: 'Raja Sahib'. 6-time Chief Minister. Longest serving CM. From Bushahr royal family. (वीरभद्र सिंह - 6 बार मुख्यमंत्री).
  • Shanta Kumar: First non-Congress CM (1977). 'Pani Wala CM'. Writer ('Dharti Hai Balidan Ki'). From Palampur. (शांता कुमार - पानी वाला सीएम).
  • Satyanand Stokes: American missionary (Samuel Evans Stokes). Introduced Apple cultivation in Kotgarh (1918). Fought for India's freedom. (सत्यानंद स्टोक्स - सेब).
  • Thakur Ram Lal: Second CM of HP. Also served as Governor of Andhra Pradesh. (राम लाल).

6. Sports Persons (खिलाड़ी)

Icons who brought glory to the state and nation.
राज्य और राष्ट्र का नाम रोशन करने वाले।

  • The Great Khali (Dalip Singh Rana): WWE Wrestler. From Dhiraina (Sirmaur). First Indian world champion in WWE. (द ग्रेट खली - सिरमौर).
  • Vijay Kumar: Shooter. Silver Medal in London Olympics (2012). From Hamirpur. (विजय कुमार - शूटिंग).
  • Ajay Thakur: Kabaddi player. Captain of Indian team. Padma Shri awardee. From Nalagarh (Solan). (अजय ठाकुर - कबड्डी).
  • Shiva Keshavan: Luge pilot. 6-time Olympian. From Manali. 'Fastest Indian on Ice'. (शिवा केशवन - ल्यूज).

7. Social Reformers (समाज सुधारक)

Individuals who worked for social upliftment.
सामाजिक उत्थान के लिए कार्य करने वाले।

  • Kinkri Devi: Environmentalist from Sirmaur. Fought against illegal mining. Received 'Jhansi Ki Rani Lakshmi Bai Stree Shakti Puraskar'. (किंकरी देवी - पर्यावरण).
  • Gambhari Devi: Folk singer from Bilaspur. Preserved folk culture. Inspiration for many. (गंभरी देवी - लोक गायिका).
  • Lal Chand Prarthi: 'Kulut Desh Ki Kahani'. Social worker and minister from Kullu. Promoted Pahari language. (लाल चंद प्रार्थी).
  • Baba Bhalku: Guided British engineers for Kalka-Shimla Railway track alignment using his stick. (बाबा भलकू - रेलवे).

8. Administrative Figures (प्रशासनिक हस्तियां)

Key officials in HP history.
हिमाचल के इतिहास में प्रमुख अधिकारी।

  • N.C. Mehta: First Chief Commissioner of HP (1948). (एन.सी. मेहता).
  • S. Chakravarti: First Governor of HP (1971). (एस. चक्रवर्ती).
  • Justice M.H. Beg: First Chief Justice of HP High Court (1971). Later CJI. (एम.एच. बेग).
  • Justice Leela Seth: First Woman Chief Justice of a High Court in India (HP High Court, 1991). (लीला सेठ).
  • Lt. Gen K.S. Katoch: First Chairman of HP Public Service Commission. (के.एस. कटोच).

9. Contemporary Icons (समकालीन प्रतीक)

Modern achievers making headlines.
सुर्खियों में रहने वाले आधुनिक अचीवर्स।

  • Kangana Ranaut: Bollywood Actress. National Award winner. From Bhambla (Mandi). (कंगना रनौत - अभिनेत्री).
  • Preity Zinta: Bollywood Actress. From Rohru (Shimla). (प्रीति जिंटा).
  • Mohit Chauhan: Playback Singer. Famous for Silk Route band. From Nahan (Sirmaur). (मोहित चौहान - गायक).
  • Anupam Kher: Actor. Studied in Shimla. (अनुपम खेर).
  • Yami Gautam: Actress. From Bilaspur. (यामी गौतम).
