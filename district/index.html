Detailed District Notes (विस्तृत जिला नोट्स)

In-depth analysis of all 12 districts of Himachal Pradesh.
(हिमाचल प्रदेश के सभी 12 जिलों का गहन विश्लेषण।)

1. Kangra (कांगड़ा)

Ancient 'Trigarta'. Largest district by population (15.10 Lakh - 2011). HQ: Dharamshala (Winter Capital of HP).
प्राचीन 'त्रिगर्त'। जनसंख्या के हिसाब से सबसे बड़ा जिला। मुख्यालय: धर्मशाला।

  • History: Ruled by Katoch dynasty (Founded by Susharma Chand). Brajeshwari temple looted by Mahmud Ghazni (1009). Sansar Chand's golden era. 1905 Earthquake devastated Kangra. (इतिहास - कटोच वंश, 1905 भूकंप).
  • Geography: Dhauladhar range acts as a backdrop. Beas river flows through. Pong Dam (Maharana Pratap Sagar) - Ramsar site. Tea cultivation in Palampur. (भूगोल - धौलाधार, पोंग बांध).
  • Culture: Kangra School of Painting (Radha-Krishna theme). Masroor Rock Cut Temple (Monolithic). Jwalamukhi (Eternal flame), Chamunda Devi, Baijnath (Shiva) temples. (संस्कृति - कांगड़ा चित्रकला, मसरूर).
  • Economy: Agriculture (Wheat, Rice), Tourism (McLeodganj - Dalai Lama's abode), Tea industry. (अर्थव्यवस्था).

2. Mandi (मंडी)

Formed by merger of Mandi and Suket states (15 April 1948). 'Chhoti Kashi' (81 temples). HQ: Mandi.
मंडी और सुकेत रियासतों के विलय से बना। 'छोटी काशी'।

  • History: Mandi founded by Ajbar Sen (1527). Suket founded by Bir Sen (765 AD). Raja Ishwari Sen was imprisoned by Sansar Chand for 12 years. (इतिहास - अजबर सेन, ईश्वरी सेन).
  • Geography: Beas river flows through Mandi town. Balh valley (Most fertile in HP). Rewalsar Lake (Sacred to Hindus, Buddhists, Sikhs). Kamrunag Lake. (भूगोल - व्यास, रिवालसर, कमरुनाग).
  • Fair: International Shivratri Fair (Padal Ground). Started by Ajbar Sen. Hundreds of deities assemble. (मेला - शिवरात्रि).
  • Modern: IIT Mandi at Kamand. Rock salt mines at Gumma/Drang (Only in India after partition). (आधुनिक - आईआईटी, नमक की खानें).

3. Shimla (शिमला)

Capital of HP. Erstwhile 'Mahasu' district. Summer Capital of British India (1864-1947). HQ: Shimla.
हिमाचल की राजधानी। पूर्ववर्ती 'महासू' जिला।

  • History: Discovered by Lt. Ross (1819). 'Kennedy House' first pucca house (1822). Municipal Corp formed 1852 (Oldest in Himalayas). Gandhi Ji visited Shimla 10 times. (इतिहास - ले. रॉस, नगर निगम).
  • Geography: Built on Seven Hills (Jakhu, Prospect, Observatory, Summer, Elysium, Inverarm, Bantony). Satluj, Pabbar, Giri rivers. Chanshal Peak (4520m). (भूगोल - सात पहाड़ियाँ, चांशल).
  • Economy: Apple bowl of India (Kotgarh - Satyanand Stokes). Tourism (Kufri, Narkanda, Rohru). Potato Research Institute (CPRI) at Bemloi. (अर्थव्यवस्था - सेब, पर्यटन).
  • Heritage: Kalka-Shimla Railway (UNESCO World Heritage). Viceregal Lodge (IIAS). Christ Church (Ridge). (विरासत - रेलवे, चर्च).

4. Chamba (चंबा)

Ancient 'Champa'. Preserved ancient culture due to geographic isolation. HQ: Chamba (Right bank of Ravi).
प्राचीन 'चंपा'। अलगाव के कारण प्राचीन संस्कृति संरक्षित।

  • History: Founded by Raja Sahil Varman (920 AD), named after daughter Champavati. Meru Varman (680 AD) built ancient temples in Bharmour (Brahmpura). (इतिहास - साहिल वर्मन, मेरु वर्मन).
  • Geography: Ravi and Chenab (Pangi) rivers flow through. Pir Panjal and Dhauladhar ranges. Mani Mahesh Lake (Abode of Shiva). (भूगोल - रावी, चिनाब, मणिमहेश).
  • Culture: Minjar Fair (Maize flowering). Chamba Rumal (Embroidery - GI Tag). Chamba Chappal. Gaddi and Gujjar tribes. (संस्कृति - मिंजर, रुमाल, गद्दी).
  • Places: Khajjiar ('Mini Switzerland' - labeled by Willy P. Blazer in 1992). Bharmour (84 Temples). Pangi Valley (Remote). (स्थान - खज्जियार, पांगी).

5. Kullu (कुल्लू)

Ancient 'Kuluta'. Valley of Gods (Dev Ghati). Famous for International Dussehra. HQ: Kullu.
'कुलूत'। देवताओं की घाटी। दशहरे के लिए प्रसिद्ध।

  • History: Founded by Vihangamani Pal. Capital shifted from Jagatsukh to Naggar to Sultanpur. Mentioned in Ramayana/Mahabharata. (इतिहास - विहंगमणि पाल).
  • Geography: Beas river flows centrally. Parvati valley. Great Himalayan National Park (UNESCO). Rohtang Pass connects to Lahaul. (भूगोल - व्यास, GHNP).
  • Culture: International Kullu Dussehra (Raghunath Ji yatra - No Ravan burning). Hadimba Temple (Manali - Pagoda style). Malana (Oldest Democracy - Jamlu Devta). (संस्कृति - दशहरा, मलाणा).
  • Economy: Horticulture (Apple, Plum), Handloom (Kullu Shawls/Caps), Tourism (Manali, Kasol, Manikaran). (अर्थव्यवस्था - शॉल, पर्यटन).

6. Solan (सोलन)

'Mushroom City of India'. Industrial hub of HP. Created on 1 Sept 1972. HQ: Solan.
'भारत का खुम्ब शहर'। हिमाचल का औद्योगिक केंद्र।

  • History: Comprised of Baghal, Baghat, Kunihar, Kuthar states. Named after Goddess Shoolini. Arki was capital of Baghal. (इतिहास - शूलिनी माता, अर्की).
  • Economy: Baddi-Barotiwala-Nalagarh (BBN) is Asia's Pharma Hub. Solan (Mushroom, Tomato - 'Red Gold'). (अर्थव्यवस्था - बद्दी, खुम्ब, टमाटर).
  • Places: Kasauli (Cantt established 1842), Chail (Highest Cricket Ground, Palace), Mohan Shakti Heritage Park. (स्थान - कसौली, चायल).
  • Institutes: Dr. Y.S. Parmar University of Horticulture and Forestry (Nauni - First in Asia). Central Research Institute (CRI) Kasauli. (संस्थान - नौणी विश्वविद्यालय).

7. Sirmaur (सिरमौर)

Southern-most district. 'Peach Bowl' (Rajgarh). Trans-Giri area (Haati community). HQ: Nahan.
सबसे दक्षिणी जिला। 'आड़ू का कटोरा'। मुख्यालय: नाहन।

  • History: Founded by Raja Rasaloo/Shubhansh Prakash (1195). Nahan founded by Karam Prakash (1621). Raja Shamsher Singh built Nahan Fort. (इतिहास - रसालू, कर्म प्रकाश).
  • Geography: Divided by Giri river into Cis-Giri and Trans-Giri. Churdhar peak (Highest in Shivalik). Renuka Lake (Largest natural lake - Shape of sleeping woman). (भूगोल - गिरी, रेणुका).
  • Economy: Ginger (Largest producer in HP), Peach, Garlic. Paonta Sahib industrial area. (अर्थव्यवस्था - अदरक, आड़ू).
  • Culture: Renuka Fair (Mother Renuka & Son Parshuram meeting). Haati community (Tribal status demand). (संस्कृति - रेणुका मेला, हाटी).

8. Lahaul & Spiti (लाहौल और स्पीति)

Largest district by area (13,835 sq km - 24.85% of HP). 'Land of Lamas'. Cold Desert. HQ: Keylong.
क्षेत्रफल में सबसे बड़ा। 'लामाओं की भूमि'। शीत मरुस्थल।

  • Geography: Separated by Kunzum Pass. Chenab (Chandra-Bhaga at Tandi) and Spiti rivers. Pin Valley National Park. Baralacha La. (भूगोल - कुंजुम, पिन घाटी).
  • Culture: Tibetan Buddhism. Tabo Monastery (996 AD - Ajanta of Himalayas), Key Monastery. Halda (Lahaul) and Losar (Spiti) festivals. (संस्कृति - ताबो, लोसर).
  • Economy: Seed Potato (Disease free), Kuth (Medicinal), Green Peas. Highest Post Office (Hikkim). (अर्थव्यवस्था - बीज आलू, कुठ).
  • Access: Atal Tunnel (Rohtang) provides all-weather connectivity to Lahaul valley. (पहुंच - अटल टनल).

9. Kinnaur (किन्नौर)

'Land of Gods' (Kinnar Desh). Formed on 1st May 1960 (from Mahasu). HQ: Reckong Peo.
'किन्नर देश'। 1960 में गठित। मुख्यालय: रिकांग पिओ।

  • Geography: Satluj river (enters India at Shipki La). Kinner Kailash peak (Sacred Shivling). Sangla (Baspa) valley - most beautiful. (भूगोल - सतलुज, सांगला).
  • Culture: Polyandry (Pandava marriage). Phulaich (Flower festival). Kinnauri Topi (Green velvet). Grokch (Village oracle). (संस्कृति - बहुपति, फुलाइच).
  • Economy: Apples (Kalpa is famous), Chilgoza (Neoza - only place in India), Almonds. Hydro power (Nathpa Jhakri, Karcham Wangtoo). (अर्थव्यवस्था - सेब, चिलगोज़ा).
  • History: Part of Bushahr state originally. Known as 'Chini' tehsil. Description in Kalidasa's Kumarasambhava. (इतिहास - बुशहर, चीनी).

10. Bilaspur (बिलासपुर)

Known as 'Kahlur' state. Submerged partially by Bhakra Dam. HQ: Bilaspur.
'कहल्लूर' रियासत। भाखड़ा बांध।

  • History: Founded by Bir Chand (697 AD). Capital shifted to Bilaspur by Dip Chand (1654). Merged into HP on 1 July 1954. (इतिहास - बीर चंद).
  • Geography: Satluj river divides district. Govind Sagar Lake (Largest artificial lake). Seven hills (Sat Dhar). (भूगोल - सतलुज, गोविंद सागर).
  • Culture: Nalwari Fair (Cattle fair) started by W. Goldstein (1889). Naina Devi Temple (Shakti Peeth). (संस्कृति - नलवाड़ी, नैना देवी).
  • Economy: Cement (ACC Barmana). Fishery in Govind Sagar. (अर्थव्यवस्था - सीमेंट, मत्स्य).

11. Hamirpur (हमीरपुर)

Smallest district (1118 sq km). Highest literacy rate. Carved out of Kangra (1972). HQ: Hamirpur.
सबसे छोटा जिला। साक्षरता दर सर्वाधिक।

  • History: Named after Raja Hamir Chand (1700-1740). Battle of Mahal Morian (Sansar Chand vs Gorkhas) fought here. (इतिहास - हमीर चंद, महल मोरियां).
  • Geography: Shivalik hills. Beas river forms northern boundary. Gasota Mahadev. (भूगोल - शिवालिक).
  • Education: 'Education Hub' of HP. NIT Hamirpur (Originally REC). HP Technical University. (शिक्षा - एनआईटी).
  • Important Places: Deotsidh (Baba Balak Nath Temple), Nadaun (Nadaun Battle), Sujanpur Tira (Holi festival). (स्थान - दियोटसिद्ध, सुजानपुर).

12. Una (ऊना)

Gateway to Himachal. 'Jaswan' state. Only district with broad gauge railway. HQ: Una.
हिमाचल का प्रवेश द्वार। 'जस्वां' रियासत।

  • History: Part of Kangra till 1966. Jaswan Dun founded by Purab Chand. Kutlehar state. (इतिहास - पूरब चंद).
  • Geography: Swan River ('River of Sorrows' - now tamed). Shivalik hills. Bordering Punjab. (भूगोल - स्वान नदी).
  • Culture: Chintpurni Temple (Shakti Peeth). Baba Barbhag Singh Mela (Mairi). (संस्कृति - चिंतपूर्णी, बड़भाग सिंह).
  • Economy: Industrial areas (Mehatpur, Tahliwal, Amb). Agriculture (Wheat, Maize). (अर्थव्यवस्था - उद्योग).
