Complete Guide to Himachal Pradesh GK

A comprehensive resource covering the Geography, History, Economy, and Culture of the "Land of Gods" for competitive exams.

Geography & Terrain

Explore the three zones: Shivalik, Mid-Himalayas, and Great Himalayas. Master the river systems of Satluj, Beas, Ravi, Chenab, and Yamuna.

  • Major Passes: Rohtang, Shipki La
  • Glaciers: Bara Shigri, Sonapani
  • Hot Springs: Manikaran, Tattapani

History & Heritage

Trace the history from ancient Trigarta to the Gorkha invasion (1805). Learn about the Prajamandal movement and the formation of HP.

  • Katoch Dynasty & Sansar Chand
  • Formation of HP (1948)
  • Statehood Day (1971)

Culture & Customs

Himachal is known for its distinct Pahari culture. Study the architectural styles of temples (Pagoda, Nagara) and famous fairs like Minjar and Lavi.

  • Temples: Hidimba, Jwalaji
  • Dances: Nati, Dangi
  • Tribes: Gaddis, Kinnauras

Economy & Dev

Understand the backbone of HP's economy: Hydroelectric Power and Horticulture. Learn about major projects like Nathpa Jhakri.

  • "Apple State of India" status
  • Hydro Power Potential
  • Tourism & Ecotourism

Polity & Admin

Overview of the administrative structure, 12 districts, Vidhan Sabha (68 seats), and the High Court. Key constitutional history.

  • Administrative Divisions
  • Lok Sabha (4) & Rajya Sabha (3)
  • First CM: Dr. Y.S. Parmar

Famous People

Notable personalities from Himachal Pradesh in the fields of defense (Param Vir Chakra), sports, literature, and arts.

  • Major Somnath Sharma (PVC)
  • Captain Vikram Batra (PVC)
  • Sardar Shobha Singh (Artist)
