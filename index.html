Essential Facts & Insights

Key topics covered in our detailed study modules.

Geography & Terrain

Explore the physical features of the 'Abode of Snow'.
हिमाचल की भौगोलिक विशेषताओं का अन्वेषण करें।

  • Three Zones: Outer Himalayas (Shivalik), Lesser Himalayas (Dhauladhar/Pir Panjal), Great Himalayas (Zaskar).
  • River Systems: Satluj (Longest), Beas, Ravi, Chenab (Largest by volume), Yamuna (Shortest).
  • High Peaks: Reo Purgyil (6816m), Shilla (6132m), Manirang (6597m).
  • Glaciers: Bara Shigri (Largest), Sonapani, Gyephang, Lady of Keylong.

History & Heritage

From ancient Trigarta to the 18th State of India.
प्राचीन त्रिगर्त से भारत के 18वें राज्य तक।

  • Ancient: Trigarta (Kangra), Kuluta (Kullu), Audumbaras, Kunindas.
  • Medieval: Mahmud Ghazni's invasion (1009), Sansar Chand's Golden Age, Gorkha Wars (1805-1815).
  • British Era: Anglo-Gorkha War, Treaty of Sugauli, Shimla as Summer Capital (1864).
  • Modern: Prajamandal Movement, Dhami Firing (1939), Statehood (25 Jan 1971).

Culture & Customs

A vibrant tapestry of fairs, festivals, and architecture.
मेलों, त्योहारों और वास्तुकला की एक जीवंत टेपेस्ट्री।

  • Temple Styles: Pent-roof (Lakshana Devi), Pagoda (Hadimba), Nagara (Masroor), Dome (Jwalamukhi).
  • Fairs: Minjar (Chamba), Lavi (Rampur - Trade), Shivratri (Mandi), Dussehra (Kullu).
  • Dances: Nati (Guinness Record), Dangi, Chham (Mask Dance), Thoda (War Dance).
  • Tribes: Gaddis, Gujjars, Kinnauras, Pangwals, Swanglas.

Economy & Dev

Hydropower and Horticulture are the twin pillars.
पनबिजली और बागवानी दो मुख्य स्तंभ हैं।

  • Hydropower: Potential ~27,436 MW. Major projects: Nathpa Jhakri (1500 MW), Bhakra, Pong, Chamera.
  • Agriculture: 'Apple State of India'. First apples by Satyanand Stokes (Kotgarh).
  • Industries: Pharma hub (Baddi-Barotiwala-Nalagarh). Cement plants (Barmana, Darlaghat).
  • Schemes: HIMCAD, Mukhya Mantri Khet Sanrakshan, Prakritik Kheti Khushhal Kisan.

Polity & Admin

Administrative evolution and constitutional framework.
प्रशासनिक विकास और संवैधानिक ढांचा।

  • Evolution: Chief Commissioner's Province (1948) -> Part C State (1951) -> UT (1956) -> State (1971).
  • Assembly: 68 Seats. First Speaker: Jaiwant Ram. First CM: Dr. Y.S. Parmar.
  • Admin: 12 Districts, 3 Divisions (Kangra, Mandi, Shimla). High Court est. 1971 (M.H. Beg).
  • Local Bodies: Panchayati Raj Act 1994. 3-tier system.

Districts Profile

Unique facts about all 12 districts.
सभी 12 जिलों के बारे में अनूठे तथ्य।

  • Kangra: Most populous. Tea capital. Masroor Temple.
  • Lahaul-Spiti: Largest area. Lowest population density. Cold Desert. Potato farming.
  • Hamirpur: Highest literacy. Highest road density. Smallest area.
  • Mandi: 'Chhoti Kashi'. IIT Mandi. Salt Mines (Gumma/Drang).
  • Chamba: Ravi river. Minjar fair. Switzerland of HP (Khajjiar).

Famous People

Legends who shaped the state and nation.
राज्य और राष्ट्र को आकार देने वाले महापुरूष।

  • Defense: Major Somnath Sharma (First PVC), Capt. Vikram Batra (PVC), Riffleman Sanjay Kumar (PVC).
  • Politics: Dr. Y.S. Parmar (Architect of HP), Virbhadra Singh (6-time CM).
  • Arts/Lit: Sardar Sobha Singh (Painter), Norah Richards (Theatre), Ruskin Bond (Kasauli connection).
  • Sports: The Great Khali, Vijay Kumar (Shooter), Charanjit Singh (Hockey).

Flora & Fauna

Biodiversity, National Parks, and State Symbols.
जैव विविधता, राष्ट्रीय उद्यान और राज्य प्रतीक।

  • State Symbols: Animal: Snow Leopard. Bird: Western Tragopan (Jujurana). Flower: Pink Rhododendron. Tree: Deodar.
  • National Parks: Great Himalayan NP (UNESCO), Pin Valley, Khirganga, Inderkilla, Simbalbara.
  • Sanctuaries: Kibber (Largest), Shilli (Smallest), Pong Dam (Bird), Renuka.
  • Herbs: Kuth, Patish, Kala Zira (GI Tag).

Tourism

Destinations, adventure, and spiritual circuits.
गंतव्य, रोमांच और आध्यात्मिक सर्किट।

  • Hill Stations: Shimla, Manali, Dalhousie, Dharamshala, Kasauli.
  • Adventure: Paragliding (Bir Billing), Skiing (Solang, Kufri), Rafting (Beas, Sutlej).
  • Religious: Shakti Peethas (Jwalamukhi, Naina Devi), Buddhist Monasteries (Tabo, Key), Sikh Gurudwaras (Paonta, Manikaran).
  • Heritage: Kalka-Shimla Railway (UNESCO), Viceregal Lodge, Naggar Castle.

Famous Tribes

Indigenous communities and their unique culture.
स्वदेशी समुदाय और उनकी अनूठी संस्कृति।

  • Gaddis: Shepherds of Chamba/Kangra. Worship Shiva. Wear Chola-Dora.
  • Kinnauras: Inhabitants of Kinnaur. Practice polyandry (historically). Speak Kinnauri.
  • Gujjars: Pastoral nomads. Hindu & Muslim Gujjars. Buffalo rearers.
  • Pangwals: Pangi Valley (Chamba). Famous for 'Jhanjhar' dance.
