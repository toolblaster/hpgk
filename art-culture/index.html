Detailed Culture Notes (विस्तृत संस्कृति नोट्स)

Comprehensive guide to the rich heritage of Devbhoomi.
(देवभूमि की समृद्ध विरासत का व्यापक गाइड।)

1. Temple Architecture (मंदिर वास्तुकला)

Himachal is known as 'Devbhoomi'. The architecture reflects the influence of local materials and history.
हिमाचल को 'देवभूमि' के नाम से जाना जाता है, जहाँ वास्तुकला में स्थानीय सामग्रियों और इतिहास का प्रभाव दिखता है।

  • Pent-Roof (Chalet Style): Oldest style. Indigenous to hills. Examples: Lakshana Devi (Bharmour - 650 AD, by Meru Varman), Shakti Devi (Chatrari). (पेंट-रूफ - लक्षणा देवी, शक्ति देवी - सबसे पुरानी शैली).
  • Pagoda Style: Multi-tiered roofs (3-4 tiers). Influence of Nepal/Tibet. Examples: Hidimba Devi (Manali - 1553 AD by Bahadur Singh), Parashar Rishi (Mandi), Sungra Maheshwar (Kinnaur). (पैगोडा - हिडिम्बा, पराशर).
  • Nagara (Shikhara) Style: Curvilinear towers. Examples: Masroor Rock Cut Temple (Kangra - 8th Century, 'Ellora of Himalayas'), Baijnath (1204 AD), Lakshmi Narayan (Chamba). (नागर - मसरूर, बैजनाथ).
  • Pyramidal Style: Hateshwari Devi (Hatkoti - Jubbal), Shiva Temple (Hatkoti). (पिरामिडल - हाटकोटी).
  • Dome Style: Sikh/Mughal influence. Jwalamukhi, Chintpurni, Brajeshwari (Kangra). (गुंबद - ज्वालामुखी, चिंतपूर्णी).

2. Famous Fairs (प्रसिद्ध मेले)

Fairs are socio-economic gatherings. 7 fairs have 'International' status.
मेले सामाजिक-आर्थिक मिलन स्थल हैं। 7 मेलों को 'अंतर्राष्ट्रीय' दर्जा प्राप्त है।

  • Minjar Mela (Chamba): Starts 2nd Sunday of Shravan (Aug). Celebrates maize flowering. Lord Raghunath & Varuna worshipped. Started by Raja Sahil Varman (935 AD) on victory over Kangra. (मिंजर - चंबा, मक्का, वरुण देव).
  • Lavi Fair (Rampur): Oldest trade fair (300+ years). Signed treaty between Bushahr & Tibet (17th Century). Famous for Chamurthi Horse, Pashmina, Dry fruits. Starts 11 Nov. (लवी - रामपुर, व्यापारिक मेला).
  • Kullu Dussehra: International. Starts on Vijayadashami (when it ends elsewhere). Continues for 7 days at Dhalpur Maidan. Lord Raghunath's idol brought from Sultanpur. (कुल्लू दशहरा - ढालपुर मैदान).
  • Shivratri (Mandi): International. Started by Raja Ajbar Sen (1527 AD). Padal ground. 200+ deities assemble to pay homage to Madho Rao (Lord Vishnu). (शिवरात्रि - मंडी, पड्डल मैदान).
  • Renuka Fair (Sirmaur): Celebrates meeting of Lord Parshuram with mother Renuka. Held 10 days after Diwali (Prabodhini Ekadashi). (रेणुका मेला - परशुराम और रेणुका).

3. Unique Festivals (अनूठे त्यौहार)

Festivals marking seasons, harvest, and local legends.
मौसम, फसल और स्थानीय किंवदंतियों को चिह्नित करने वाले त्यौहार।

  • Phulaich (Ukhayang): 'Festival of Flowers' in Kinnaur (Sept). Villagers go to high hills to collect flowers (Brahmakamal) to offer to village deity. (फुलाइच - किन्नौर, फूलों का त्यौहार).
  • Halda: Lahaul's New Year (Magh month - Jan). Cedar twigs torched (Shishkarapa). Similar to Diwali but without lights. (हालदा - लाहौल का नया साल).
  • Losar: Tibetan New Year celebrated in Lahaul-Spiti and Kinnaur. Mask dances (Chham) performed. (लोसर - तिब्बती नया साल).
  • Sair: 'Bada Tyohar' in Kangra/Mandi (Sept). Marks end of rainy season. Offering of crops/walnuts. Barber shows mirror. Bull fights held at Arki. (सैर - कांगड़ा/मंडी, सांडों की लड़ाई).
  • Nalwari (Bilaspur): Cattle fair started by W. Goldstein (1889). Famous for wrestling (Chhinj). Held in March at Luhnu ground. (नलवाड़ी - बिलासपुर, पशु मेला).

4. Folk Dances (लोक नृत्य)

Vibrant expressions of joy, performed during fairs and weddings.
खुशी की जीवंत अभिव्यक्ति, मेलों और शादियों के दौरान।

  • Nati: Most popular dance of HP (Kullu/Shimla/Sirmaur). Listed in Guinness Book (2016) as largest folk dance (9892 women participated). (नाटी - सबसे लोकप्रिय, गिनीज रिकॉर्ड).
  • Dangi & Deepak: Chamba (Gaddi tribe). Dangi performed by women at Chhatrari jatar. Deepak is a group dance. (डांगी और दीपक - चंबा).
  • Chham (Devil Dance): Lahaul-Spiti/Kinnaur monasteries (Kye, Tabo). Masked dance by Lamas to ward off evil spirits. (छम - मुखौटा नृत्य).
  • Thoda: War dance of Sirmaur/Shimla. Archery game depicting war between Kauravas (Sathi) vs Pandavas (Pashi). (ठोडा - युद्ध नृत्य, तीरंदाजी).
  • Jhamakda: Kangra. Performed by women during marriage ceremonies. Songs are full of humor. (झमाकड़ा - कांगड़ा, विवाह गीत).

5. Pahari Painting Schools (पहाड़ी चित्रकला)

World-renowned miniature paintings. Metcalfe called it "Combines the cool of the hills with the fire of the plains".
विश्व-विख्यात लघु चित्रकला।

  • Basohli School: Oldest school (17th Century). Bold colors, fish-shaped eyes, receding foreheads. Patron: Raja Kripal Pal. Main artist: Devidas (Painted Rasamanjari). (बसोहली - सबसे पुरानी, चटक रंग).
  • Kangra School: Golden age under Maharaja Sansar Chand. Theme: Radha-Krishna (Shringar Rasa), Geet Govind, Bhagwat Purana. Artists: Fattu, Padma, Nainsukh. Delicate lines. (कांगड़ा - संसार चंद, राधा-कृष्ण).
  • Arki School: Baghal state. Basohli influence initially, then Kangra style. Famous for wall paintings in Arki Fort. (अर्की - बाघल, भित्ति चित्र).
  • Chamba Rumal: Pictorial embroidery on handkerchiefs ("Paintings with needle"). GI Tagged. Theme: Raasmandal, Rukmini Haran. Promoted by Raja Umed Singh. (चंबा रुमाल - सुई से चित्रकला).

6. Languages & Scripts (भाषाएँ और लिपियाँ)

Linguistic diversity of the Western Pahari region.
पश्चिमी पहाड़ी क्षेत्र की भाषाई विविधता।

  • Tankri Script: Old script of Pahari language, derived from Sharada script. Used in business records and royal farmans. (टांकरी लिपि - शारदा से व्युत्पन्न).
  • Dialects:
    • Mandyali: Mandi (Literary works exist).
    • Chambyali: Chamba.
    • Kehloori: Bilaspur (Resembles Punjabi).
    • Baghati: Solan.
    • Bhoti: Lahaul-Spiti/Kinnaur (Tibetan origin). (प्रमुख बोलियाँ).
  • Literature: 'Chamba Painting' (V.C. Ohri), 'Kangra Valley Painting' (M.S. Randhawa). Dr. Y.S. Parmar wrote 'Polyandry in the Himalayas'. 'Numismatics History of HP' by P.L. Gupta. (साहित्य - रंधावा, ओहरी).

7. Social Customs & Marriage (सामाजिक रीति-रिवाज)

Unique social fabric of hill tribes.
पहाड़ी जनजातियों का अनूठा सामाजिक ताना-बाना।

  • Polyandry: Prevalent in Kinnaur/Lahaul (Pandava marriage). Fraternal polyandry (brothers share wife) to prevent land division. (बहुपति प्रथा - भूमि विभाजन रोकने के लिए).
  • Reet: Old custom of divorce/separation and remarriage by paying money (Reet money) to the first husband. Abolished by British. (रीत - तलाक और पुनर्विवाह).
  • Jhanjrara/Gaddar: Marriage by elopement or mutual consent (Gaddis/Shimla). Less ritualistic, no Saptapadi. Nose ring ceremony is key. (झंझराड़ा/गदर).
  • Batt-Satta: Exchange marriage system where a bride is taken in exchange for giving a bride from one's own family. (बट्टा-सट्टा - विनिमय विवाह).
  • Dharm Puthra: Ritual brotherhood/sisterhood established between individuals of different castes. (धर्म पुत्र).

8. Tribes & Traditional Attire (जनजातियाँ और वेशभूषा)

Distinct identity of Himachali people.
हिमाचली लोगों की विशिष्ट पहचान।

  • Gaddis: Shepherds of Bharmaur/Kangra. Semi-nomadic. Wear 'Chola' (Gown) and 'Dora' (Black Rope). Worship Lord Shiva (Gabbar). (गद्दी - चोला और डोरा).
  • Kinnauras: Kinnaur. Wear 'Thepang' (Green velvet Cap). Legend says they are descendants of Kinnaras (Mythical beings with horse head). (किन्नौरा - थेपांग टोपी).
  • Pangwals: Pangi valley (Chamba). Famous for 'Jhoori' songs. Women perform 'Pangwal' dance. (पांगवाल).
  • Swangla: Lahaul (Pattan valley). Social hierarchy: Brahmins/Rajputs (Bodhi) and lower castes. Marriage types: Rusia-Byah (Elopement). (स्वांगला).
  • Attire: Luanchari (Gaddi women dress), Dhatu (Square Headscarf in Shimla/Kullu), Loia (Mens woolen sheet in Sirmaur/Shimla), Pullas (Grass shoes). (वेशभूषा - लुआंचड़ी, ढाटू, लोईया).

9. Heritage Sites & Institutions (विरासत स्थल)

Preserving the legacy of the past.
अतीत की विरासत को संरक्षित करना।

  • UNESCO World Heritage: Kalka-Shimla Railway (Listed 2008), Great Himalayan National Park (Listed 2014). (यूनेस्को - कालका-शिमला रेलवे, GHNP).
  • Tabo Monastery: 'Ajanta of Himalayas'. Lahaul-Spiti. Founded in 996 AD by Ringchen Zangpo. Oldest functioning monastery. (ताबो मठ - हिमालय का अजंता).
  • Viceregal Lodge (IIAS): Shimla. Designed by Henry Irwin (Jacobethan style). Completed 1888. Summer retreat of Viceroy. Now IIAS. (वायसराय लॉज).
  • Nicholas Roerich Art Gallery: Naggar (Kullu). Russian artist who made HP his home. Famous for painting Himalayas. (रोरिख आर्ट गैलरी).
  • Andretta Pottery: Kangra. Started by Norah Richards (Irish writer) and Sardar Gurcharan Singh. Famous artist colony. (अंद्रेटा पॉटरी).
