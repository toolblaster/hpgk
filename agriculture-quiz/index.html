Comprehensive Agriculture Guide (व्यापक कृषि गाइड)

A complete study resource covering agricultural practices, natural resources, industries, and infrastructure essential for competitive exams.
(प्रतियोगी परीक्षाओं के लिए आवश्यक कृषि प्रथाओं, प्राकृतिक संसाधनों और बुनियादी ढांचे को कवर करने वाला एक संपूर्ण अध्ययन संसाधन।)

1. General Agriculture (सामान्य कृषि)

Foundational concepts of agriculture, agricultural economics, and extension education. (कृषि, कृषि अर्थशास्त्र और विस्तार शिक्षा की मूलभूत अवधारणाएं।)

  • Agricultural Revolutions (Green, White, Blue).
  • Important agricultural institutes and their locations.
  • Basic terminologies and historical developments.

2. Agronomy, Forestry & Horti. (शस्य, वानिकी, बागवानी)

Detailed study of crop management, forest ecosystems, and orchard planning. (फसल प्रबंधन, वन पारिस्थितिकी तंत्र और बाग नियोजन का विस्तृत अध्ययन।)

  • Weed management and seed technology.
  • Silviculture and agroforestry practices.
  • Cultivation of fruits, vegetables, and ornamental plants.

3. Soil Science (मृदा विज्ञान)

Understanding soil health, types, and chemical composition. (मिट्टी के स्वास्थ्य, प्रकार और रासायनिक संरचना को समझना।)

  • Soil formation (Pedogenesis) and profile.
  • Physical and chemical properties of soil.
  • Manures, fertilizers, and nutrient management.

4. Cropping Patterns (फसल चक्र)

Analysis of agricultural seasons and crop rotations across India. (कृषि मौसमों और भारत भर में फसल चक्रों का विश्लेषण।)

  • Rabi, Kharif, and Zaid cropping seasons.
  • Intercropping, mixed cropping, and relay cropping.
  • Climatic requirements for major crops (Rice, Wheat).

5. Irrigation (सिंचाई)

Water resource management and modern irrigation techniques. (जल संसाधन प्रबंधन और आधुनिक सिंचाई तकनीकें।)

  • Surface, sub-surface, and micro-irrigation methods.
  • Water use efficiency and consumptive use.
  • Drainage systems to prevent waterlogging.

6. Farming Systems (कृषि प्रणालियां)

Different approaches to farming and agricultural enterprises. (खेती और कृषि उद्यमों के विभिन्न दृष्टिकोण।)

  • Subsistence, intensive, and extensive farming.
  • Integrated Farming Systems (IFS) and organic farming.
  • Dryland agriculture and sustainable practices.
